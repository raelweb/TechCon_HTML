TechCon: A Brief History

TechCon started in 2010 as a small gathering of tech enthusiasts and innovators. Its goal was simple: bring together developers, entrepreneurs, and industry leaders to share ideas, explore emerging technologies, and foster collaboration. Over the years, TechCon has grown into one of the premier technology conferences in the world, attracting thousands of participants from diverse industries. From its humble beginnings with a few dozen attendees, the conference now features:


Keynote speeches from top tech visionaries
Workshops and hands-on labs for cutting-edge technologies
Networking sessions connecting startups, investors, and professionals
Innovation showcases highlighting the latest in AI, robotics, and software development

Our Mission

TechCon aims to inspire, educate, and connect the technology community. Our goals are to foster innovation, encourage collaboration, and empower individuals to shape the future of technology. We believe in making knowledge accessible, building a supportive community, and showcasing transformative technologies.

Past Speakers

TechCon has hosted numerous notable speakers who have made significant contributions to the tech industry. Here are a few highlights:

Faisal Mushtaq — Founder & CEO, TechCon SoCal

Faisal Mushtaq is the founder and CEO of TechCon SoCal, a leading technology and innovation conference that brought together hundreds of tech professionals, investors, entrepreneurs, and researchers in 2024. Under his leadership, the event showcased insights on digital transformation, startup innovation, and strategic vision for emerging technologies. Faisal’s career spans leadership in digital strategy, R&D, and guiding tech startups toward sustainable growth.

Aniqa Hijazi — Co-Founder, TechCon SoCal & Tech Innovator

Aniqa Hijazi is a co-founder of TechCon SoCal and a respected voice in the tech community for her work building platforms that support innovation and entrepreneurship. She has played a key role in shaping the direction of TechCon events, creating spaces where rapid technological advancements and business collaboration can thrive. Her work focuses on empowering emerging tech leaders and fostering partnerships across sectors.

Joe Kiani — Founder, Masimo

Joe Kiani is the founder of Masimo, a leading global medical technology company known for pioneering industry-leading innovations in noninvasive patient monitoring. As a speaker at TechCon in 2024, Joe shared insights into how technology can revolutionize healthcare systems, improve patient outcomes, and accelerate the adoption of data-driven tech solutions. His experience bridges deep technical knowledge with strategic business leadership.
